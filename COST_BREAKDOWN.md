- **Estimated Annual**: ₹0-24,000/year

#### SMS Gateway (Twilio/AWS SNS)
- **Cost per SMS**: ₹0.50-1.00
- **Estimated volume**: 10,000 SMSs/month
- **Estimated Annual**: ₹60,000-120,000/year

#### Shiprocket/Delhivery
- **Pickup charges**: ₹20-30 per shipment
- **API**: Free
- **Estimated Annual**: Embedded in logistics costs

#### Image Storage (Optional - Cloudinary/S3)
- **Cloudinary Free**: 25 credits/month (enough for 1000 images)
